Dream Symbolism Overview:

Water in Dreams:
- Represents emotions and the unconscious mind
- Calm water suggests peace and tranquility
- Turbulent water indicates emotional upheaval

Falling Dreams:
- Often symbolize loss of control
- May represent anxiety or insecurity
- Can indicate a transition or significant life change

Flying Dreams:
- Symbolize freedom and liberation
- Represent overcoming limitations
- Suggest personal empowerment and aspiration
